About TEDx RWTHAachen

Our mission is to spread ideas for a better future and plant seeds in our engaging community with fresh ways of seeing the world. It’s a meeting place where we all have a chance to think critically on today’s society, reflect deeply on what’s happening in our culture, education, technology, and encourage each other to take action. Join us to chase the extraordinary stories that need to be shared!
