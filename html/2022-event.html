2022 UNCHARTED WATERS

[11th of June 2022, Technologiezentrum Aachen, Attendees: 400]

With every milimetre we chart of the unknown depth, the ocean floor sinks miles further beneath us—and you never know what you’ll find under the waters. Curiosity has been and still remains the instinctive motivation that drives mankind forward, both in our individual growth as well as in our collective advances. Generations of restless explorations and innovations, we have seen the unimaginable becomes daily reality. Yet every millimetre we venture below, miles and miles the ocean floor sinks beneath us. With the theme Uncharted Waters, TEDxRWTHAachen is delighted to welcome you at a full-day TEDx experience to explore and question novel perspectives. Where will the currents turn? When rises the tide of the next pivotal change, one that will rip our world apart then build it anew? Are we headed towards the brilliance of a guiding star, or the white-hot burning of a warning?
